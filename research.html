Research

Massive Galaxy Quenching

My work investigates the nature of dark energy by studying large-scale structures and cosmic expansion using observational data and theoretical modeling.

Ram-Pressure Stripping in Act

I explore how galaxies evolve over cosmic time, focusing on star formation at high redshifts and environmental effects in cluster and field galaxies.

Chemical Evolution of Galaxies

My research includes the analysis of metallicity gradients and chemical enrichment processes in nearby galaxies, helping to trace their star formation history.
