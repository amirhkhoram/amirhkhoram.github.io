About Me

I'm a Persian PhD candidate in Astronomy at the University of Bologna, where I explore the universe through the lenses of astrophysics and cosmology. My fascination with the stars began when I was just 6 years old—and it’s been a thrilling journey ever since.

I thrive on tackling diverse research challenges across multiple areas of science. Whether it’s galaxies, dark energy, or cosmic structure, I believe that juggling interdisciplinary projects fuels creativity and innovation. I’m also passionate about astrophotography—capturing galaxies, nebulae, and the night sky through my lens is where science meets art for me.

Outside academia, I’m a poet, gravel biker, and an experimental home chef. I find joy in discovering new cuisines from different cultures, just as much as I do in riding long remote trails or crafting a well-timed verse. For me, curiosity doesn’t stop at the edge of the cosmos—it’s a way of life.
